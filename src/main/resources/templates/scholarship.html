Financial Support Options

We believe that financial barriers should never stand in the way of creative excellence. Our Institute provides diverse support mechanisms to ensure inclusivity.

Support Type Description
Financial Aid Programme Designed for students with genuine financial need. Assistance includes fee concessions and structured instalment plans.
Army & SC/ST Support Dedicated support for defence families and inclusive education for SC/ST communities.
Loan Assistance Expert guidance on securing education loans and documentation support.

State or national level sports achievers are eligible for concessions in the first academic year.

General Guidelines
  • Concession percentages are not cumulative; only one concession applies at a time.
  • Concessions apply only to tuition fees, not hostel or miscellaneous charges.
  • Students must declare eligibility at the time of admission and provide valid supporting documents.
