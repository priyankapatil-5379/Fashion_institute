Admissions & Enrollment

You can apply directly through our website by clicking the "Apply Now" button on the course details page. Alternatively, you can visit our campus or connect with an enrollment advisor via our inquiry form.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Courses & Curriculum

Yes, we have various short-term certificate programs in Embroidery, Crochet, Nail Art, and Fabric Painting designed for working professionals and hobbyists.

Our curriculum is developed in collaboration with top fashion designers and industry experts to ensure students learn current trends, technologies, and professional workflows.

Career & Placements

We have a dedicated placement cell that organizes campus interviews, industry visits, and portfolio showcases. Our students are often placed with top fashion houses, retail giants, and creative agencies.
